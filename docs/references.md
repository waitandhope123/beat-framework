## Core Modern-State Data

- **Cross-National Time-Series Data Archive (CNTS)**  
  Banks, A. S., et al.  
  Domestic conflict and political event data.

- **Polity Project / Polity5**  
  Center for Systemic Peace.  
  Regime authority characteristics and transitions.

- **Correlates of War (COW) Project**  
  - State System Membership (Independent States Since 1815)  
  - Intra-State Wars  
  - Intra-State War Participants  
  Comprehensive data on state systems and conflict.

- **National Material Capabilities (NMC)**  
  Correlates of War Project.  
  Measures of relative state capabilities.

---

## Governance and Institutional Data

- **Quality of Government (QoG) Standard Dataset**  
  University of Gothenburg.  
  Governance and institutional quality indicators.

- **V-Dem / DemocracyData**  
  Varieties of Democracy Project; DemocracyData (Marquez).  
  Supplementary regime and democracy indicators.

---

## Historical and Long-Run Data

- **Maddison Project Database (MPD)**  
  Long-run economic and population estimates.

- **Global Historical Population Datasets**  
  Long-term population reconstructions.

---

## Environmental and Contextual Data

- **Paleoclimate and Environmental Reconstructions**  
  Regional temperature and climate proxy datasets
  used for contextual interpretation of ancient and pre-modern cases.

---

## Notes

- Inclusion in this list does not imply redistribution.
- BEAT does not depend on any single dataset.
- Historical and environmental sources were used for
  comparative and contextual validation only.
